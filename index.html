Aura is a professionally trained makeup artist. She began her career in 2013 assisting and training under several well known professionals in the beauty industry. She went on to graduate from Make Up For Ever Academy Paris and continues her training attending yearly Makeup Artistry Seminars and Workshops. Her work has been seen on TV, print, and the faces of beautiful brides all over the world. She also serves as a Makeup Educator holding popular beauty seminars and one-on-one classes. She is skilled, well-informed, as well as passionate about her craft, and this has helped her achieve her success.
